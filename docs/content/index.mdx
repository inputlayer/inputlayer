# InputLayer

**Define facts and rules. Get derived conclusions that stay correct as your data changes - with a full audit trail for every result.**

InputLayer follows chains of facts to reach answers that search alone can't. You define relationships - compatibility, ownership, access policies - and the engine derives what follows. When a fact changes, only the affected conclusions update. When you need to know why, every result traces back to the rules and facts that produced it.
